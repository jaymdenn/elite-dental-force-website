Flexible Paths to Get Started

Choose the engagement model that fits your practice's needs and timeline.

Subscription Access

Full platform access with standard onboarding for practices ready to get started quickly.

  • Self-service setup with guided tutorials
  • Email and chat support
  • Access to knowledge base and training videos
  • Monthly pricing with no long-term commitment

Best for: Single-location practices with technical familiarity

Guided Implementation

Dedicated implementation support to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

  • Assigned implementation specialist
  • Custom configuration and workflow setup
  • Live training sessions for your team
  • Go-live support and validation

Best for: Multi-location groups wanting comprehensive support

Enterprise Onboarding

White-glove implementation for DSOs and large organizations with complex requirements.

  • Dedicated project manager
  • Custom integrations and API configuration
  • Phased rollout across locations
  • Executive stakeholder alignment

Best for: DSOs and enterprise organizations

Pilot Program

Test the platform with a subset of your organization before full deployment.

  • Limited-scope implementation
  • Defined success metrics
  • ROI analysis and reporting
  • Clear path to full deployment

Best for: Organizations wanting to validate before committing
