Building the Future of Dental Revenue Intelligence

Our Mission

Eliminate Revenue Uncertainty

We believe dental practices shouldn't have to guess about eligibility, wrestle with denials, or struggle with inconsistent workflows. Our mission is to provide the infrastructure and intelligence that transforms revenue operations from a source of friction into a competitive advantage.

Our Vision

Infrastructure Leadership

We envision a future where every dental practice, from single-location offices to enterprise DSOs, has access to the same caliber of revenue intelligence that was once reserved for the largest healthcare organizations. We're building that future.

Security

Security & Compliance

Protecting your practice and patient data is our highest priority.

HIPAA-Aligned Architecture

Elite Dental Force maintains rigorous security practices aligned with HIPAA requirements for protected health information.

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256)
  • SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Role-based access controls
  • Complete audit logging
  • Regular third-party penetration testing
  • Business Associate Agreements available
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Elite Dental Force.

Elite Dental Force is a dental revenue intelligence platform that combines AI-powered consultants with the EDiFi software platform to help practices optimize eligibility verification, reduce claim denials, and improve overall revenue performance.
We maintain HIPAA-aligned security practices including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 Type II certification, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
Elite Dental Force serves practices of all sizes, from single-location private practices to multi-state DSOs with hundreds of locations. Our platform scales to meet the needs of any organization.
EDiFi integrates with leading practice management systems including Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve, Denticon, and many others. We also connect directly with major clearinghouses and payers.
The best way to get started is to book a demo with our team. We'll walk you through the platform, understand your specific needs, and recommend the best engagement path for your practice.
