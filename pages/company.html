About Elite Dental Force

Empowering Your Practice with AI Solutions

We're building the infrastructure that transforms dental revenue operations from uncertainty to confidence.

Our Founder

Alvin Uta'i

Founder & CEO

Alvin is an expert in AI-driven dental billing transformation, specializing in clean-claim automation, denial prevention, and scalable workflows. With deep roots in revenue cycle management, he recognized that dental practices were drowning in administrative complexity while leaving significant revenue on the table.

His vision for Elite Dental Force was born from firsthand experience watching practices struggle with eligibility verification errors, mounting claim denials, and inefficient manual processes. Alvin set out to build an intelligent platform that eliminates these pain points through AI-powered automation and real-time analytics.

Under his leadership, EDF has pioneered solutions that help practices achieve clean-claim rates exceeding 98%, reduce denial rates by up to 40%, and scale their operations without adding administrative overhead. Alvin's commitment to innovation continues to drive EDF's mission: empowering dental practices with the technology they need to thrive.

Our Mission

From Chaos to Clarity

We believe dental practices shouldn't struggle with eligibility guesswork, claim denials, or inconsistent workflows. Our mission is to provide intelligent infrastructure that transforms revenue operations into a competitive advantage.
