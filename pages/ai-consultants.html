What They Are

Intelligent Partners for Your Team

AI Consultants are specialized AI assistants trained to understand dental revenue workflows and provide contextual guidance to every team member.

What AI Consultants Do

Our AI Consultants serve as always-available experts that guide your team through complex decisions, ensuring consistent execution across your organization.

  • Provide real-time guidance on eligibility verification and coverage questions
  • Suggest optimal coding and documentation for maximum reimbursement
  • Standardize workflows so every team member follows best practices
  • Accelerate onboarding with contextual training and support

What They Are Not

AI Consultants are decision support tools that enhance your team's capabilities. They do not replace human judgment or professional expertise.

  • They are not a replacement for licensed professionals or clinical judgment
  • They do not make autonomous decisions without human oversight
  • They are not medical or legal advisors
  • They do not store or share patient data inappropriately
