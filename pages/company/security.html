Security & Compliance

Your Data Security Is Our Priority

Elite Dental Force maintains enterprise-grade security practices to protect your practice and patient data at every level.

HIPAA Compliance

HIPAA-Aligned Architecture

Elite Dental Force maintains rigorous security practices aligned with HIPAA requirements for protected health information. Our infrastructure is designed from the ground up to meet and exceed healthcare data protection standards.

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256)
  • SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Role-based access controls
  • Complete audit logging
  • Regular third-party penetration testing
  • Business Associate Agreements available
Security Framework

Enterprise-Grade Protection

Our comprehensive security framework covers every aspect of data protection.

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. TLS 1.3 protects all communications between systems.

Access Control

Role-based access controls ensure users only access the data they need. Multi-factor authentication is available for all accounts.

Audit Logging

Comprehensive audit trails track all system access and data modifications. Logs are retained according to healthcare compliance requirements.

Continuous Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring detects and responds to potential threats in real-time. Automated alerts notify our security team of anomalies.

Infrastructure Security

Our cloud infrastructure is hosted in SOC 2 certified data centers with physical security controls, redundant power, and disaster recovery.

Vulnerability Management

Regular penetration testing by third-party security firms. Continuous vulnerability scanning and rapid patch deployment.

Certifications

Compliance & Standards

We maintain industry-recognized certifications and adhere to healthcare compliance standards.

HIPAA Compliance

Healthcare Data Protection

Our platform is designed to support HIPAA compliance requirements. We provide Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) and maintain technical, administrative, and physical safeguards for protected health information.

SOC 2 Type II

Service Organization Controls

Annual SOC 2 Type II audits verify our security, availability, and confidentiality controls. Reports are available to customers under NDA upon request.

Business Partners

Business Associate Agreements

Elite Dental Force provides Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) to all customers as part of our standard service. Our BAA outlines our responsibilities for protecting PHI and demonstrates our commitment to HIPAA compliance.

Request BAA Information

Our BAA Covers:

  • Permitted uses and disclosures of PHI
  • Safeguards for protecting PHI
  • Breach notification procedures
  • Subcontractor requirements
  • Audit and compliance verification
